Best Time to Go Mactan

Dry and Summer Season

When going to Mactan, it's best to plan your visit from the months of December to May. A lot of important events happen during this time as well, and there is less humidity compared to other months.This is also the dry season, so you can get to enjoy the outdoor activities fully and water adventures that Mactan has in store for you.

Kadaugan sa Mactan

During April, you can celebrate the Kadaugan sa Mactan in Lapu-Lapu City with the Oponganons. It depicts the fall of Magellan against Lapu Lapu, the first native who resisted against the colonization of the Spaniards.
